Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated: Jun 28, 2023

Thank you for using our services. We strive to provide the best experience possible. However, we understand that circumstances may change, and you may need to cancel your subscription or request a refund. Please review the following policy regarding cancellations and refunds:

Subscription Cancellation

  1. You may cancel your subscription at any time by following the cancellation procedure outlined in your account settings or by contacting our support team.
  2. Upon cancellation, your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period. You will not be billed for any subsequent periods.

Refund Eligibility

  1. Refunds are only available for premium subscription payments made within the last 3 days
  2. To be eligible for a refund, you must provide a valid reason for the cancellation of your subscription and demonstrate that our service did not meet your expectations or function as intended.
  3. Refunds are not provided for partial subscription periods or for any time remaining on your current billing cycle.
  4. If you received any promotional or discounted pricing, the refund amount will be adjusted accordingly to account for the discounted rate.

Refund Process

  1. To request a refund, please contact our support team and provide the necessary details, including your account information, reason for cancellation, and any supporting documentation if required.
  2. Our team will review your refund request and respond to you within 7 days to inform you of the status of your refund.
  3. If your refund request is approved, the refund will be processed using the original payment method within 14 days. Please note that it may take additional time for the refund to be reflected in your account.

Exceptions

  1. We reserve the right to refuse a refund if it is determined that you have violated our terms of service or engaged in any fraudulent activity.
  2. Refunds are not provided for any third-party fees, such as transaction fees or charges imposed by payment processors.
  3. This refund policy only applies to subscriptions purchased directly from us. If you have subscribed through a third-party platform or service, please refer to their respective refund policies.
